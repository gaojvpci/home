Why do I love Math?

I love math because you can do so many useful things with it. For example I used Trigonometry in a diep.io simulation program to rotate the tank and to shoot and move projectiles. I also like to solve problems in math because it gives me a sense of accomplishment and makes me feel smart. You can also use Calculus (or at least what I know of calculus) to calculate the area under a function, to, for example, calculate the balance of a bank account where each y value is the amount of money deposited into the account at one time (by using lots and lots of rectangles and adding their areas).
